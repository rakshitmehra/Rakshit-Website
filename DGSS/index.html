Who We Are

A Society made by the Students, For the Students & Of the Students of Department of Computational statistics and data analytics, Guru Nanak Dev University. Through this society, the students will get to explore themselves and their fields of interest. Also, they will get various opportunities to be a part of several events that will be held within and around the department, in the university campus. Moreover, its a great way to make new friends, try out new activities.

Future Plans:

We will focus on welfare of our fellow students and try to make them a better version of themselves.

We will organize various Technical, Cultural, Literary, Creative and other events related to finance and stock market analysis. We will also try to help students regarding the placement activities like building a better skill set that will make their CVs look more impressive. Also, we will plan various fun events and fests to make your campus life memorable.

Society Clubs

Technical Club
This club will be responsible for making students more aware about new technologies. All tech-related events will be organized under this club.
Placement Club
This club will give students information about the placements and also help them build new skills and a better CV.
Literary Club
This club will be responsible for making students more aware of the literature and also help them build confidence and enhance their storytelling skills.
Finance and Stock Market Club
This club will help you get insights into finance and the stock market and the technologies related to it.
Cultural and Creative Club
This club will help u enhance your creativity and cultural values by being a part of various events organized by them.
Sports Club
this club will be responsible for organizing all sports events and other outdoor activities. It will also help students practice new sports.
